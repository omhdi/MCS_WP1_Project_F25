Policies & Standards

Service Policy

EventHub is committed to providing accurate, up-to-date event information. Organizers are responsible for the content they publish. We reserve the right to remove events that violate community guidelines, contain misleading information, or promote harmful activities.

  • All events must include accurate date, time, and location details.
  • Discriminatory or offensive content is strictly prohibited.
  • Users may report suspicious or inappropriate listings.

Publication Standards

We maintain high editorial standards to ensure quality listings across the platform. Every submitted event is reviewed for completeness and compliance before publication.

  • Event descriptions must be clear, factual, and free of spam.
  • Images must be relevant and appropriately licensed.
  • Contact information must be valid and responsive.
  • Updates to published events should be made promptly.
